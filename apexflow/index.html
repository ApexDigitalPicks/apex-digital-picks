The problem

The lead that messages at 21:00 does not wait until Monday.

Most SA businesses run on WhatsApp, but nobody can answer around the clock. Generic chatbots are worse: one invented price or made-up discount and you are stuck honouring it. ApexFlow was built the other way around, accuracy first, human in control.

What it does

+Answers questions, accurately

Replies come only from your knowledge base: your prices, policies and hours. The agent is blocked from inventing anything that is not in it. Anything unclear goes to a human.

+Qualifies every lead

Collects name, company, what they need and when, naturally in conversation. Leads appear in your dashboard ready to follow up.

+Drafts quotes with a PDF

Built from your price list only, with VAT breakdown, on your letterhead. One tap to approve and the customer receives it in WhatsApp.

+Takes booking requests

Gathers dates, items and site details. It never confirms a booking as final. Your team always makes the call.

+Speaks 4 languages

English, Afrikaans, isiZulu and Sepedi. The agent replies in whichever language the customer writes or speaks.

+Understands voice notes

Voice notes are transcribed and handled like text. If a note cannot be understood, the agent politely asks the customer to type it.

How it works

1

2

3

4

POPIA built in: consent notice on first contact, instant STOP opt-out handled by a human, and all customer data stored on your own server in South Africa. No third party analytics.

Questions owners ask us

What stops it saying something wrong to my customers?

Three things. It may only use facts from your own knowledge base and price list. If a customer asks about anything not covered, it hands over to a human instead of guessing. And by default every single reply waits in your dashboard until you approve it.

Will my customers know it is AI?

Yes, by design. The first message identifies it as an AI assistant, as POPIA good practice requires, and if a customer asks, it says so. Trust is the product.

What do I need to get started?

A WhatsApp Business number, your price list and policies (we help you structure them), and about a week for setup and testing. You review replies from your phone or laptop.

What does it not do?

It does not check live stock or calendar availability out of the box, it does not confirm bookings as final, and it does not process images yet. Complex custom quotes always route to your team. If your business needs system integrations, ask us about it in the demo.
