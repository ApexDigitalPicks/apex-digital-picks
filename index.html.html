Frequently asked

Is Apex Digital Picks free to use?
Yes, completely free. We earn a small commission when you purchase a product through our links — at no extra cost to you. This is how we keep the lights on while keeping our content free.
Are your reviews genuinely independent?
Absolutely. We only recommend products we've personally tested. We don't accept payment for positive reviews, and we always disclose our affiliate relationships upfront.
Do the products cost extra if I buy through your links?
Never. Affiliate links cost you exactly the same as going directly to the vendor. Sometimes we even negotiate exclusive discounts for our readers.
How often do you update your reviews?
All reviews are updated at least every 90 days. Software changes fast — we stay on top of pricing, feature updates, and new alternatives so your information is always current.
Can I suggest a product for review?
Yes! We love reader suggestions. Join our newsletter and reply to any email with your suggestion — we read every one and add the best candidates to our review queue.
